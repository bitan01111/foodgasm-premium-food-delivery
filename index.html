📜 Terms & Privacy Policy

Terms & Conditions

By using Foodgasm, you agree to our terms of service. Orders placed are binding and subject to restaurant availability. Foodgasm is not responsible for third-party restaurant quality or delays beyond our control. Pricing may vary and is inclusive of applicable GST.

Privacy Policy

We collect personal information (name, email, phone, delivery address) solely to process your orders and improve our services. Your data is encrypted and never sold to third parties. Location data is used only for delivery purposes and is not stored beyond your session unless you opt-in. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support.

Cancellation & Refund

Orders may be cancelled within 2 minutes of placement. After food preparation begins, cancellations are at the restaurant's discretion. Refunds for eligible cancellations are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
